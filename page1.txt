Executive Summary
Early childhood education management platforms streamline operations for daycares, preschools, and childcare centers by digitizing communication, attendance, billing, and compliance processes. The market opportunity is significant as traditional centers still rely heavily on paper-based systems and generic tools, creating inefficiencies and compliance risks.
